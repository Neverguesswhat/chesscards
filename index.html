Card Chess
Chess rules. To move you must play 1 piece card + 1 direction card. Direction is from your perspective (Black forward = down).
Turn: White
🎯
Flow: pick a piece card → pick a direction card → click a matching piece → click a highlighted square.
If you're stuck: use Redraw Pieces or Redraw Directions (costs your turn).
Your Hand
White: 5 piece cards + 5 direction cards
Piece Cards (required)
Direction Cards (required)
Direction cards only filter which chess-legal moves you can use. Example: a pawn needs Forward to advance (unless it can capture diagonally).
Battle Log
